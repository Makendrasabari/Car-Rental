Clarifications

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the rental documents required?

For Indian nationals, a valid Driving License, Aadhaar Card, and Passport or PAN Card copy are required. For foreign nationals, an International Driving Permit (IDP) and a copy of their Passport and Visa are mandatory.

Is security deposit mandatory?

Yes, a refundable security deposit is held before vehicle delivery. The amount depends on the car category (e.g. higher for supercars like Lamborghini) and is refunded within 48 hours post rental completion.

Can I opt for self-drive?

Yes, self-drive options are available for sports and premium SUV categories. However, ultra-luxury sedans like the Rolls-Royce Phantom are strictly available with a Stackly professional chauffeur only.

How do you calculate rental days?

Rental duration is calculated on a 24-hour cycle. Pick-up and drop-off extensions are permitted with advance coordination, subject to hourly rates if they exceed grace periods.
