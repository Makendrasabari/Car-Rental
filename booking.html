Booking FAQs

What documents are required?

For self-drive options, you must upload a valid driving license (domestic or international permits) alongside government photo ID such as Aadhar or passport credentials.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes. A security deposit is pre-authorized on your credit card upon confirmation. The hold is fully released within 48 business hours after safe return of the vehicle.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made up to 24 hours prior to the scheduled pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations inside 24 hours incur a single-day rental charge.

Do you offer door-to-door delivery?

Yes, we deliver luxury vehicles directly to your residence, hotel, or private airport terminal across Salem, Chennai, and Coimbatore.
